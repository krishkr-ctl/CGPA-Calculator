How to calculate CGPA?

Calculating your Cumulative Grade Point Average (CGPA) is essential for tracking your academic progress in engineering college. Follow these simple steps:

CGPA = Σ (Credit × Grade Point) / Σ Credits

Step 1: List all your subjects along with their credit hours.

Step 2: Enter the grade points you obtained for each subject (usually on a scale of 0 to 10).

Step 3: Multiply the credit hours by the grade point for each subject.

Step 4: Sum up all the products and divide by the total number of credits.
